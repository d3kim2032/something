Welcome!

This version of the website includes CSS styling built right into the HTML. It features a modern card-based layout, a responsive design that works beautifully on mobile devices, and even adapts to your system's dark or light mode settings automatically.

Design Features Added

  • Custom Fonts: Uses clean, highly readable system fonts instead of the default browser serif font.
  • Color Palette: A visually appealing combination of neutral backgrounds with a bold indigo primary accent.
  • Spacing & Layout: Centered content with ample whitespace (padding and margins) to let the text breathe.
  • Cards & Shadows: Content is housed inside rounded containers with subtle drop shadows to create a sense of depth.
  • Interactive States: Hover effects on cards and buttons make the page feel alive and responsive to your mouse.
